Welcome to Apartment services!
Through this website you can create your user account with your apartment number, name, and phone number.
Once you have created your account, you can take various actions:

  • File service tickets for repairs
  • File complaints as needed or respond to complaints
  • Check service ticket status
  • Read about the history of this apartment complex!
